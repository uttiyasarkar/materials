Comments from Prof. Sanjay Swain

1. P8: Why v is there ? Isn‚Äôt supposed to be normalized state of (phi 1 +/- phi 2)?

This expression of field is at the ground state where the energy is minimum after SSB. So, phi_1 is v and phi_2 is zero. I have changed the field notation to \eta and \xi to avoid confusion.

2. P11. Table 2.1: How do you experimentally measure the charge of quarks ? For that matter even how to experimentally find our quark have spin=1/2 and has 3 colours?

There is no direct measurement of the fractional charge of the quarks, since quarks are never free and they are always in the bound states. So to experimentally determine the fractional charge, color and spin of the quarks, we look for processes like electron-positron anhiliation. The annihilation can produce muon-antimuon pairs or quark-antiquark pairs which in turn produce hadrons. The hadron events are evidence of quark production and the process itself describes the spin 1/2 nature of the quarks. The ratio of the number of hadron events to the number of muon events gives a measure of the number of "colors" of the quarks. If the ratio were 1, then there would have been no color charge. The evidence points to five quarks with three colors.

3. P11. Table 2.2: Is there reason why range of weak interaction (10^-16) is smaller than the one mentioned below (which is for strong interaction ~ 10 ^-13 as shown by you)?

The range depends on the energy scale of the theory. Weak interaction is a short range interaction where the parameter of the theory depends on W/Z mass, whereas the strong interaction makes sense at the nucleus of an atom which is about 10^-13 cm in radius.

4. P12: What is meaning of unification ? The three couplings should be exactly same or they should be related or something else?

Here, by unification we meant that all three fundamental forces can be described by a common theory which includes different couplings and term from all three forces. There can be unified theories where couplings are same at some high energy and the symmetry of the theory is broken at some energy scale.
